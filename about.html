About Us

SCHOOL CURRICULUM

School session begins from April and ends in the month of March. School is an English Medium School. As we focus more on English because it is an International Language and is used in every parts of the world. Hindi is also taught as it is the Mother Toungue.
All the course of studies is based on the rules according to C.B.S.E. (Central Board of Secondary Education)
All the subject under the carriculum of the school are as follows:
English, Hindi, Science, Mathematics, Social Studies, Sanskrit, EVS, Moral Science, Value Education, Computer Science and General Knowledge.
Extra Co-Curriculum Activities:
Competition: Elocution, Recitation, Debate, Quiz, Creative Writting, General Knowledge, Current Affairs, Drawing, Crafts, Singing, Fancy Dress, Dance, Yoga, Meditation, Sports, Rangoli competition, Mehandi Competition etc.

VISION POINTS

  1. Academic Empowerment through comprehensive curriculum.
  2. Emphasis on nurturing character, personality, life values and vocational skills.
  3. Develop critical and logical thinking skills.
  4. Promote Social, Emotional, Cultural, Mental and Physical growth.
  5. Develop Positive self-esteem.
  6. Train children to be eco-friendly. ("EACH ONE PLANT ONE")
  7. Build up leadership and Entrepreneurial Excellence.
  8. Unleash creative abilities and communication skills and use them productively.
  9. To provide best quality education in affordable fees.
  10. Provide Knowledge for all subjects through the medium of English Language.

Rules and Regulations

  • Students are to maintain a high standard of good manner and general behavior.
  • They should be polite and well-behaved within the school compound and outside and nuture qualities of respect for elders and teachers. Honesty and truthfulness at all time and places.
  • Students are exhorted to be orderly. Clean in person and mind. Neatly dressed. Well-Behaved at all time and co-operate in maintaining the cleanliness of the school class room and campus.
  • Absolute silence must be observed during the general assembly, while moving into classroom and in the absence of teachers. During the recess students will not be have rudely and violently.
  • In the absence of teachers, the Monitor assume full responsibility for maintenance of order and discipline in the class.
  • Students who are given any responsibility should under take it dutifully and impartially in accordance with the directions of the Principle or teacher deputed by him.
  • Students must not walk about in the verandah and around the classroom during the class hours without any important work.
  • Students should not write, carve or scratch on the school walls or deface the school furniture.
  • Respect for oneâ€™s property and the school property as well as those belonging to other is the mark of the well-behaved and responsible student. Damage of any kind to the school property will be made good from student.
  • Students must always speak in English in the school premises and maintain a high standard in their conversations.
  • All the students must be in possession of a copy of the school Handbook & Diary which must be brought to the school daily.
  • Each student is responsible for his/her things and the school will not be responsible for the loss of books, money, pencils, erasers and other accessories of the student. It is advisable not to bring any valuable things to school.
  • Students are strictly prohibited from bringing into school campus books. Comics, Newspapers, Pictures, Periodicals, Instruments or Gadgets of any kind not pertaining to their diary work and not approved by the Principle.
  • Articles found in the school premises are to be handed over to the office.
  • Students must not throw about bits of paper or food stuff anywhere in the school premises. Except in the bins provided for the purpose.
  • Pupils are not allowed to enter the staff room unless required to do so by any teacher.
  • No student will leave the school campus on any account during the school hours.
  • Late comers and absentees from the school will not be admitted to the class without express sanction of the Principle.
  • No fines or collection for any purpose what so ever may be made without the express sanction of the Principle.
  • A collective petition or complaint will not be entertained but individual complaint will be extended sympathetic consideration.
  • No student may take private tuition in any subject from any member of the school staff.
